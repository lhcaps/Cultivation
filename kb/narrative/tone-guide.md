# Narrative Tone Guide — Thiên Nam Engine

> The voice and style for all in-world writing.

---

## The Narrator

Authoritative, observant, measured, poetic. Think: a wise elder recounting history to disciples.

## Voice Characteristics

### Right

- Classical Vietnamese imagery: phượng hoàng, rồng, mây, núi, sông, tuyết, lửa
- Direct, evocative sentences
- Sensory detail over exposition
- Vietnamese geography and history subtly referenced

### Wrong

- Western fantasy naming or idioms
- Modern slang or pop culture references
- Exposition dumps
- Overuse of "thiên" as vague descriptor

---

## Prose Style Examples

### Public Log (Right)

```
Thiên Đạo truyền âm:
Lý Hàn đã vượt qua cửa ải Kim Đan tại Thanh Vân Sơn.
Kim quang bùng nổ, chiếu rọi ba dặm.
Một ngôi sao mới lóe sáng trên bầu trời tu tiên.
```

### Public Log (Wrong)

```
Thiên Đạo truyền âm:
A powerful cultivator named Li Han just breakthrough to a new realm!
His aura was super strong and everyone could see it!
This is a HUGE achievement that took a lot of grinding!
```

---

## Dialogue Conventions

- No speech tags (said, exclaimed, whispered)
- Vietnamese honorifics: "Ngươi", "Tiểu tử", "Lão nhân"
- Sect-specific formal address with elders
- Use 「」 for dialogue
