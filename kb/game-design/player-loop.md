# Player Loop — Thiên Nam Engine

> How players spend their time in the game, organized by play session.

---

## Daily Loop (5-15 minutes)

The core daily engagement pattern. Most players complete this in under 10 minutes.

### Morning Routine

1. **`/daily`** (30 seconds)
   - Claim daily reward: 100 Ag
   - See daily event/banner
   - Quick cultivation reminder

2. **`/cultivate`** (1-3 minutes)
   - Pick cultivation mode (usually Stable or Sect)
   - Click button, see result
   - Progress bar update

3. **`/profile`** (30 seconds, if needed)
   - Quick stats check
   - See if breakthrough is close

4. **`/mission`** (1-2 minutes, if available)
   - Pick up daily mission
   - Quick task (gather herbs, kill mobs)

5. **`/logs`** (optional, 30 seconds)
   - See recent action history
   - Good for tracking progress

### Total: ~5-10 minutes active play

---

## Engagement Sessions (30-60 minutes)

Longer sessions for deeper engagement. Players do multiple activities.

### Session A: Cultivation Focus

1. **`/cultivate`** x2 (10 minutes)
   - Two cultivation sessions
   - Maybe try Forced for bonus

2. **`/encounter`** (5 minutes)
   - Random encounter
   - Make choice

3. **`/profile`** (2 minutes)
   - Check progress
   - Plan next session

### Session B: Adventure Focus

1. **`/travel`** to new region (2 minutes)
   - Explore different area
   - Different encounters

2. **`/encounter`** x2 (10 minutes)
   - Multiple encounters in new region
   - Region-specific events

3. **`/region`** (5 minutes)
   - Study local lore
   - Find special locations

### Session C: Social Focus

1. **`/sect view`** (3 minutes)
   - Check sect status
   - See contribution

2. **`/sect contribute`** (2 minutes)
   - Donate resources
   - Build rank

3. **Chat in Discord** (variable)
   - Roleplay with other players
   - Sect coordination

## Weekly Loop

Major milestones that reset on a weekly basis.

### Weekly Activities

| Day | Activity | Duration | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|---------|
| Monday | Sect salary | 2 min | Collect weekly pay |
| Wednesday | Boss spawn | 30 min | Server event |
| Friday | Secret realm opens | 30 min | Limited dungeon |
| Sunday | Sect meeting | 10 min | Coordination |

### Weekly Progression

By end of week, active player:
- Reaches cultivation milestone
- Completes 3-5 missions
- Attends 1 sect event
- Does 10-20 encounters
- Earns enough for minor purchase

## Seasonal Loop (30 days)

The larger rhythm of the game. Season resets create fresh starts.

### Season Start

1. Season announcement (public log)
2. New leaderboard categories
3. Available event preview
4. Player sets season goals

### Mid-Season (Day 15)

1. First major event
2. Leaderboard competition heats up
3. Sect wars begin
4. Mid-season bonus

### Season End (Day 30)

1. Season rewards distributed
2. Leaderboard finalized
3. Titles awarded
4. New season preview

## Player Archetypes

### The Casual (5-10 min/day)

- Does `/daily` and `/cultivate`
- Checks `/profile` occasionally
- Joins sect but rarely participates
- Goal: Steady progress, low commitment

### The Active (30-60 min/day)

- Completes all daily activities
- Does encounters and missions
- Active in sect events
- Competes on leaderboards
- Goal: Top 100 leaderboard

### The Dedicated (2-4 hours/day)

- Multiple cultivation sessions
- Active PvP and combat
- Manages sect
- Organizes events
- Goal: Top 10 leaderboard, sect leadership

### The Roleplayer (variable)

- Focus on narrative and RP
- Less concerned with stats
- Uses `/encounter` for story
- Builds character backstory
- Goal: Immersive experience

### The Collector (variable)

- Focus on items and manuals
- Active alchemy and crafting
- Hoards and trades
- Goal: Complete collection

## Progression Pacing

### Week 1-2: Learning

- Learn cultivation basics
- Join a sect
- First breakthrough attempt
- Explore 1-2 regions

### Week 3-4: Establishing

- Reach Luyện Hồn
- Active sect participation
- First alchemy success
- First encounter with Tà Đạo

### Month 2-3: Mid-game

- Reach Trúc Mạch
- Lead sect activities
- Participate in world events
- Compete in top 100

### Month 3+: Late-game

- Reach Kim Đan
- Sect leadership
- World boss participation
- Season champion ambitions
