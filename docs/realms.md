## 2. Realm Details

### 2.1 Luyện Thể (Mortality)

**Description**: The first realm of cultivation. Mortals begin to sense the flow of Qi in their bodies and surroundings. This is where the journey begins.

**Starting State**:
- All new characters begin here at Sơ kỳ
- Basic stats only
- Can learn 1 starter manual

**Cultivation Points**: 1,000 per sub-stage (3,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- `/cultivate` — Begin cultivation journey
- `/profile` — View own stats
- Basic combat with wild animals

**Famous Quotes**:
> *"Ta không phải kẻ thường nữa. Ta đã nhận ra khí trong cơ thể."*

---

### 2.2 Khí Tức (Breath)

**Description**: The cultivator learns to circulate Qi through their meridians. Physical strength increases dramatically. External Qi can be sensed and absorbed.

**Cultivation Points**: 3,000 per sub-stage (9,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Internal Qi circulation (passive HP regen)
- Basic pill consumption
- Can join sects
- `/breakthrough` — Attempt to advance

**Visual Indicator**: Subtle aura begins to form around the cultivator.

---

### 2.3 Luyện Hồn (Soul)

**Description**: The soul is refined and strengthened. The cultivator gains spiritual perception, allowing them to sense things beyond normal senses. Minor spiritual abilities manifest.

**Cultivation Points**: 9,000 per sub-stage (27,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Spiritual perception (enhanced encounters)
- Soul-based techniques
- `/alchemy` — Begin alchemy journey
- Sect missions unlock
- Can form party for group cultivation

**Visual Indicator**: Visible Qi aura, able to sense other cultivators.

---

### 2.4 Trúc Mạch (Foundation)

**Description**: The cultivator establishes a permanent foundation for their power. This is considered the first major milestone. Those who fail to reach this realm before age 50 rarely ever do.

**Cultivation Points**: 27,000 per sub-stage (81,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Foundation quality becomes more influential
- Can learn 3 manuals simultaneously
- `/mission` — Accept formal missions
- Can participate in sect wars
- Region-specific bonuses activate

**Famous Quotes**:
> *"Từ nay trở đi, ngươi đã thực sự bước vào con đường tu tiên."*

---

### 2.5 Kim Đan (Gold Core)

**Description**: The cultivator condenses their Qi into a Golden Core — a visible sign of true power. This is the realm of respect in the cultivation world. They are now considered senior cultivators.

**Cultivation Points**: 81,000 per sub-stage (243,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Gold Core manifestation (visual aura)
- Can establish their own sect
- Flight (short distance)
- Advanced pill creation
- Can challenge regional bosses

**Visual Indicator**: Golden core visible in dantian area. Golden Qi aura.

---

### 2.6 Nguyên Anh (Nascent Soul)

**Description**: The Gold Core hatches into a Nascent Soul that can leave the body temporarily. This is an extraordinary achievement, granting near-immortality and immense power.

**Cultivation Points**: 243,000 per sub-stage (729,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Soul projection (can leave body for short periods)
- Can create offspring/pocket dimensions
- Regional leader eligibility
- Can participate in world events
- Can establish sect headquarters

**Visual Indicator**: Nascent soul visible beside physical body. Massive golden aura.

---

### 2.7 Hóa Thần (Spirit Transmute)

**Description**: The Nascent Soul merges fully with the body. The cultivator achieves a state of spiritual unity. They can now affect the world on a spiritual level.

**Cultivation Points**: 729,000 per sub-stage (2,187,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Can manipulate spiritual energy
- Long-distance teleportation
- Can teach others at foundation level
- World event leadership
- Can participate in seasonal tournaments

**Visual Indicator**: Body and soul appear as one unified energy being.

---

### 2.8 Trú Thần (Spirit Abiding)

**Description**: The cultivator achieves a state of spiritual permanence. They are now essentially immortal unless killed. Worldly matters hold little meaning.

**Cultivation Points**: 2,187,000 per sub-stage (6,561,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Near-immortality
- Can exist in multiple locations simultaneously
- World-level influence
- Can mentor Nascent Soul cultivators
- Sect alliance leadership

---

### 2.9 Đại Thừa (Mahayana)

**Description**: The cultivator expands their spiritual self to encompass the world. They can perceive the interconnection of all things and wield tremendous power.

**Cultivation Points**: 6,561,000 per sub-stage (19,683,000 total)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Can reshape landscapes
- Control weather in local area
- Full immortality (only killed by equals or higher)
- Can ascend to higher realm with sufficient effort
- World leader candidates

**Visual Indicator**: Presence felt across an entire region. Massive spiritual pressure.

---

### 2.10 Ngũ Bất Tôn (Transcendent)

**Description**: The pinnacle of mortal cultivation. These beings exist beyond normal comprehension. They rarely appear in the mortal world, preferring the celestial realm.

**Cultivation Points**: MAX (no further advancement possible in mortal realm)

**Abilities Unlocked**:
- Full transcendence
- Can ascend beyond Thiên Nam
- Absolute power within the world
- Seasonal boss tier
- Prestige/rebirth system access

**Visual Indicator**: Presence alone can cause weaker cultivators to prostrate.

## 3. Realm Transitions

### 3.1 Sub-Stage Progression

Each realm has 3 sub-stages. Progress between sub-stages does **not** require a breakthrough ritual — it happens automatically when cultivation points are sufficient.

```
Sub-stage thresholds:
Sơ kỳ → Trung kỳ: 33% of realm total points
Trung kỳ → Hậu kỳ: 66% of realm total points
Hậu kỳ → Next realm: 100% + breakthrough required
```

### 3.2 Breakthrough Ritual

When reaching 100% of a realm's cultivation points, players can use `/breakthrough` to attempt advancement.

**Breakthrough prerequisites:**
- 100% cultivation points
- Heart demon < 80
- No severe injury (level < 3)
- Appropriate manual learned

### 3.3 Realm Lock

Some content is locked by realm:

| Content | Minimum Realm | Reason |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Join rank 3+ sects | Khí Tức | Senior enough |
| Alchemy | Luyện Hồn | Soul strength needed |
| Sect wars | Trúc Mạch | Foundation needed |
| Regional boss | Kim Đan | Power threshold |
| World boss | Nguyên Anh | Massive power needed |
| Establish sect | Kim Đan | Power threshold |
| Leaderboard season top | Trúc Mạch | Sufficient progress |

## 4. Foundation Quality

### 4.1 Overview

Foundation Quality (Căn Cơ) is a hidden stat ranging from **0 to 100** that affects multiple aspects of cultivation. It is **not** visible to players but affects all calculations.

### 4.2 Foundation Quality Effects

| FQ Range | Cultivation Speed | Breakthrough Bonus | Alchemy Bonus | Status |
|----------|------------------|-------------------|---------------|--------|
| 0-19 | -30% | -15% | -10% | Poor foundation |
| 20-39 | -15% | -5% | 0% | Average foundation |
| 40-59 | 0% | 0% | +5% | Good foundation |
| 60-79 | +10% | +5% | +10% | Excellent foundation |
| 80-89 | +20% | +10% | +15% | Superior foundation |
| 90-100 | +30% | +15% | +20% | Perfect foundation |

### 4.3 Foundation Quality Sources

| Action | FQ Change | Frequency Cap |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Breakthrough success | +3 to +6 | Unlimited |
| Thanh Tâm Đan (Thượng+) | +1 to +3 | 1/day |
| Kỳ ngộ (enlightenment) | +2 to +5 | 1/week |
| Forced breakthrough failure | -5 to -10 | Per failure |
| Severe injury | -2 to -5 | Per injury |
| Heart demon critical (100) | -10 | Per crisis |
| Realm regression | -15 | Per regression |

### 4.4 Foundation Decay

Foundation quality does **not** decay naturally. It is permanent once gained, but can be lost through catastrophic failures.
